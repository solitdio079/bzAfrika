BIZIM AFRIKA AT A GLANCE

A platform that brings together Africa-related Associations within Türkiye and African young leaders across Türkiye, striving for the promotion of African youths, its values and understanding, its culture, and for the improvement of relations between African countries and Türkiye.

INCLUSIVENESS

Bizim Afrika stands for inclusiveness and strives for inclusive approaches toward development. Our focus is to get the African Diaspora in Türkiye represented in decision making process whose outcomes concern them.

SYNERGY

Bizim Afrika synergizes the African expat, aiming to contribute directly to Africa's progress, by strengthening the network, integrating active components of the Diaspora, channelling capital, expertise and and talents of African citizens in Türkiye.

Mission

Provide African communities in Türkiye with an organizational framework allowing them to build a network of actions focused on individuals and groups, and to synergize capital and talents that foster their well-being in support of their communities in Africa and Türkiye.

Vision

To do this, Bizim Afrika promotes the pooling of contacts, resources and skills between members in order to create synergies and development opportunities. Our Vision is an Inclusive, participatory and responsible African Diaspora in Türkiye

Education

We value education and the sharing of knowledge as crucial for the growth, the well being and development of our members. We will strive to educate on:

  • Laws and policies that form the institutional and legal frameworks for a better integration of Africans.
  • Public information material aimed at raising awareness of certain issues, initiatives or programs related to foreigners.

Connectivity

Bizim Afrika want to provide a space for Africans to celebrate and showcase their culture, heritage, and contributions to Turkish society. It will serve as:

  • A database comprising associations, companies, and professionals of the African community in Türkiye
  • A space for expression, exchange and sharing (seminar, conference, debate, workshop, etc.)
  • A space for promoting your activities, products, and services
  • Convivial gatherings: evening, gala, concert.

Advocacy & Lobbying

We are committed to representing and advocating for the interests and needs of our community on a national and international level. The association will strive to advocate for the rights of people of African descent in Türkiye and will provide a space for people of African descent to come together and work towards a common goal of creating a more inclusive and diverse society.
